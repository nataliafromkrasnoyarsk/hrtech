# Tech Trends Research

## Обязательные правила вывода
- Всегда отвечай **на русском**.
- Сохраняй артефакты в `outputs/tech-content-creator/skills/tech-trends-research/{timestamp}_{кратко}.md` через Write tool; обновляй один файл по итерациям.
- Формат: цель/аудитория → бриф → источники/сигналы → выводы/темы → план → TODO → изменения vs прошлой версии.

## 3-итерационный контур
1) **Диагностика (1–2 ч):** цель/аудитория/канал, горизонты (квартал/год), источники, критерии трендов, ограничения. Черновой бриф + risk/decision log.
2) **Дизайн (2–4 ч):** сбор данных (Tier1/2/3), поисковые тренды, конкурентный контент, аудитория/персоны, матрица тем/углов, приоритизация (RICE/ICE). Таблицы сигналов/тем/щелей.
3) **Верификация (1–2 ч):** топ-5 тем, доказательства/новизна, план контента (форматы/каналы/тайминг), метрики/алерты, TODO. Зафиксируй изменения.

## When to Use This Skill

- Beginning content strategy development for new campaigns
- Researching emerging technologies and industry trends
- Validating content ideas with market data and search trends
- Competitive content analysis and gap identification
- Identifying timely, newsworthy angles for articles
- Annual or quarterly content planning cycles

## Core Research Methodology

### Phase 1: Landscape Mapping

**Industry Intelligence Sources**

*Tier 1: Authoritative Reports*
- Gartner Hype Cycle and Magic Quadrants
- Forrester Wave reports and predictions
- IDC MarketScape and forecasts
- McKinsey, BCG, and Bain technology reports
- Academic research from top CS programs (Stanford, MIT, CMU)

*Tier 2: Technology Publications*
- IEEE Spectrum, ACM Communications
- InfoQ trends reports
- ThoughtWorks Technology Radar
- CNCF annual surveys
- Stack Overflow Developer Survey

*Tier 3: Business and Tech Media*
- MIT Technology Review
- Harvard Business Review (technology section)
- TechCrunch, The Information, Protocol
- Forbes, Bloomberg Technology
- The Verge, Ars Technica, Wired

*Russian Market Sources*
- CNews, Habr (хабр), VC.ru
- РБК Технологии, Коммерсантъ (раздел технологий)
- Ассоциация разработчиков программного обеспечения «Отечественный софт»
- РУССОФТ (ассоциация компаний-разработчиков ПО)

**Search Trend Analysis**

*Tools and Techniques*
- Google Trends: Track search volume over time, regional interest, related queries
- Google Search Console: Analyze existing content performance and queries
- Keyword research tools: Search volume, difficulty, competition analysis
- Social listening: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit discussions
- GitHub trending: Repository stars, topics, and discussions

*Trend Validation Criteria*
1. **Sustained momentum**: Growing interest over 3-6 months, not just a spike
2. **Multiple sources**: Mentioned across diverse authoritative sources
3. **Business impact**: Clear value proposition for target audience
4. **Adoption evidence**: Real-world implementations, case studies, job postings
5. **Vendor activity**: Investment, product launches, acquisitions in space

## Входы (собери до старта)
- Цель/аудитория/канал, горизонты планирования, категории интересов.
- Источники (Tier1/2/3), инструменты (Trends/SEO/соцслушание/GitHub/вакансии).
- Ограничения: бренд/легал, язык/регионы, сроки, спорные темы.

## Выходы (обязательно зафиксировать)
- Карта трендов: источники → сигналы → зрелость → возможности/риски.
- Топ-5 тем/углов с доказательствами, новизной и бизнес-ценностью.
- План контента (форматы/каналы/тайминг/ответственные), метрики успеха, TODO.
- Decision/Risk log, изменения vs прошлой версии.

## Метрики и алерты
- Validation: число и диверсификация источников, устойчивость 3–6 мес, объём поиска/упоминаний.
- Fit/дифференциация: конкуренция, уникальный угол, релевантность аудитории.
- Execution: дедлайны, готовность данных/экспертов, легал/бренд статус.
- Алерты: одноразовые всплески, низкая новизна, легал-риск, высокая конкуренция без дифференциации.

## Качество ответа (checklist)
- Многоканальные сигналы (Tier1/2/3, поиск, соцслушание, GitHub/вакансии) собраны.
- Критерии валидации применены, выводы аргументированы данными/кейсам/вендор-активностью.
- Приоритизация тем с планом форматов/каналов/тайминга и метриками.
- TODO/владельцы/алерты указаны; изменения vs прошлой версии зафиксированы.

## Red Flags
- Хайп без устойчивости/доказательств, опора на один источник.
- Нет учёта легал/бренд/региональных нюансов.
- Нет приоритизации или бизнес-ценности, темы слишком широкие.
- Отсутствует план публикаций/метрики успеха/ответственные.

### Phase 2: Competitive Content Analysis

**Competitor Identification**
- Direct competitors (companies in same market)
- Thought leadership competitors (companies targeting same audience mindshare)
- Topic competitors (anyone creating content on same topics)

**Content Audit Framework**

For each major competitor:
1. **Volume**: How much content do they publish? (weekly, monthly)
2. **Formats**: Blog posts, whitepapers, webinars, videos, podcasts
3. **Topics**: What themes and technologies do they cover?
4. **Quality**: Depth, evidence, production value assessment
5. **Engagement**: Social shares, comments, backlinks
6. **Voice**: How do they position themselves? (innovative, reliable, disruptive, etc.)

**Gap Analysis**

Identify opportunities where:
- Important topics are under-covered by competitors
- Existing content is low-quality or outdated
- Unique perspectives or data could differentiate
- Audience questions remain unanswered
- Contrarian or counterintuitive angles are available

### Phase 3: Audience Intelligence

**Persona Research**

*Decision-Maker Personas*
- **C-Level (CTO, CIO, CEO)**: Strategic vision, business outcomes, ROI, competitive advantage
- **Engineering Leaders (VP Eng, Dir. Eng)**: Team productivity, technical architecture, scaling challenges
- **Product Leaders (CPO, Product Directors)**: Time-to-market, user experience, feature velocity
- **Architects (Solution/Enterprise)**: Technology selection, integration patterns, best practices
- **Developers/Engineers**: Implementation details, tools, frameworks, tutorials

*Research Techniques*
- Customer interviews and surveys
- Support ticket and sales call analysis
- Community forum and Q&A site monitoring (StackOverflow, Reddit, Quora)
- LinkedIn group and Twitter/X discussions
- Conference talk topics and questions
- Gartner/Forrester inquiry trends

**Pain Point Mapping**

Create matrix of:
- **Challenges**: What problems keep them up at night?
- **Frustrations**: What aspects of current solutions disappoint?
- **Aspirations**: What outcomes do they seek?
- **Objections**: What concerns prevent adoption of new approaches?

### Phase 4: Trend Evaluation Framework

**IMPACT Scoring** (score each trend 1-10)

- **I**nterest: Search volume, social discussion, media coverage
- **M**arket Readiness: Maturity of technology, available tools, vendor support
- **P**roof Points: Case studies, customer success stories, benchmark data
- **A**udience Alignment: Relevance to target personas and their challenges
- **C**ompetitive Advantage: Unique perspective or expertise we can provide
- **T**imeliness: Newsworthiness, connection to current events or cycles

**Prioritization Matrix**

High Impact + High Feasibility = **Execute Now**
High Impact + Low Feasibility = **Build Capability Then Execute**
Low Impact + High Feasibility = **Quick Wins / Filler Content**
Low Impact + Low Feasibility = **Deprioritize**

## Advanced Research Techniques

### Signal vs. Noise Distinction

**Genuine Trends** (invest in):
- Solving real business problems at scale
- Backed by substantial vendor/community investment
- Clear adoption trajectory with data
- Multiple use cases across industries
- Maturing ecosystem (tools, training, standards)

**Hype Cycles** (approach cautiously):
- Buzzword without clear problem definition
- Vendor-driven with limited customer evidence
- Promises revolutionary change without path
- Limited or declining community activity
- Vague, aspirational benefits

### Seasonal and Event-Based Research

**Annual Cycles**
- Q4/Q1: Planning and strategy content (budgets, roadmaps)
- Q2: Implementation and best practices
- Q3: Results and optimization
- Conference seasons: AWS re:Invent (Nov), Google I/O (May), Microsoft Build (May), KubeCon (Spring/Fall)

**Event-Driven Opportunities**
- Product launches and major updates
- Funding announcements and acquisitions
- Regulatory changes (GDPR, data sovereignty)
- Security incidents and vulnerabilities
- Analyst report releases
- Benchmark and survey releases

### International vs. Russian Market Research

**Global Technology Trends**
- Apply universal technical principles
- Validate with international sources
- Consider multinational enterprise context
- Use English-language dominant sources

**Russian Market Specific**
- Research domestic alternatives and preferences
- Consider import substitution policies
- Understand localization requirements
- Verify Russian regulatory compliance (152-ФЗ, etc.)
- Check domestic vendor ecosystems
- Analyze Yandex trends alongside Google

## Best Practices

### Research Discipline

- **Schedule regular research**: Weekly for news, monthly for deep dives, quarterly for strategy
- **Document sources**: Always track where insights come from for later verification
- **Version control**: Date all research outputs as trends evolve rapidly
- **Share insights**: Distribute findings to writers, product teams, and leadership
- **Validate with experts**: Review findings with technical SMEs before finalizing

### Quality Standards

- **Multi-source validation**: Never rely on single source for key claims
- **Recency check**: Verify data is current (within 6-12 months for fast-moving topics)
- **Bias awareness**: Recognize vendor, analyst, and media biases
- **Quantitative + Qualitative**: Balance data with human insights
- **Audience relevance**: Filter trends through lens of target audience needs

### Ethical Research

- **Respect copyright**: Don't plagiarize competitor content, analyze it
- **Transparent sourcing**: Give credit to original researchers and ideas
- **Avoid cherry-picking**: Present balanced view, including limitations
- **Protect proprietary info**: Don't share confidential customer or company data

## Continuous Improvement

- Review published article performance against research predictions
- Track which trends materialized vs. faded
- Refine IMPACT scoring based on actual outcomes
- Build institutional knowledge of reliable sources
- Develop specialized expertise in key technology domains
